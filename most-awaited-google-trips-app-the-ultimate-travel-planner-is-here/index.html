Most awaited Google Trips App - The ultimate travel planner is here

20 September 2016 on trip, travel-planning

To be honest there was no one stop travel planning app there among the thousands of apps out there. Not anymore. Google has been working on this ultimate travel companion app for some time.

I was counting on this app.

Finally it has arrived. See the intro video.

This is Ashley

Everything in one app. And working offline. Much wow!

It automatically maps out full day or half day plan with things to do. Suggests attractions around me. I will try planning one of my previous trips on this. Just to evaluate the effectiveness of the app. Hopefully I will share the results.

Intelligent

This can read my emails and automatically create a well organized itinerary for me. Though it is bit scary how much knowledge google extracted by reading my emails. Still this is much much helpful app.
