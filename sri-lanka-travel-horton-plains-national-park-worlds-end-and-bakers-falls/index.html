Sri Lanka Travel - Horton Plains National Park , World's End and Bakers Falls

20 July 2016

Visiting Sri Lanka. You love breath taking scenes? Does your body asking for freshest air? Go Horton plains national park. A protected national park in Sri Lanka highlands offer you the sight of world's end as they call it.

The park is rich in biodiversity and filled with many endemic species.

Need inspiration?

Let me share few of them.

Bakers Falls

#visitsrilanka

A photo posted by Madushan Gamage (@itzmadushan) on Jul 15, 2016 at 8:02am PDT

This is bakers falls at Horton plains. You can spend hours there.

Early bird catch the fish

Try visit Horton plains in the morning. Plan to be there around 9 O'clock in the morning. Otherwise mist would cover the best sights. In my trip I left my bungalow at Nuwara Eliya at 6:00 am to reach there early. Be prepared for rain as there is no cover in most of the places if rain falls. It just plains all around.

Be Careful

World's end - a high clifftop is a famous location for your beautiful photos. Be careful though. There can be tight winds. Your life is much more worth than your photos. You must take photos there but just be extra careful.

Remember

Please. Don't litter anything there.
