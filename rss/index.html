I hope to cover common questions about JR Pass and my personal experience about it in this post.

What is JR Pass?

JR Pass or Japanese Rail pass is a cost effective pass that allow you to use JR rail unlimited for a limited period of time. Only foreign tourists enjoy this. Residents of Japan are not allowed to have that luxury. Comes in few options where the most famous one is 7 days national JR Pass

What are the available options?

  • National JR Pass
    • 7 days
    • 14 days
    • 21 days
  • Area Passes
    • JR Kansai Pass
    • JR Hokkaido Pass
    • JR Central Pass
    • JR East Pass
    • JR Kyushu Pass
    • JR Shikoku Pass

Also there are 2 options based on which class of Shinkansen you want to travel. There is a Green Car which is kind of business class and more expensive. So pick based on your preferred level of comfort. Green Cars got more luxury seats that you can even sleep overnight.

Area passes obviously limited to an area where the usage of Shinkansen is depends on the area.

What is Shinkansen?

Well this is how they call the bullet train in Japan. You should get used to this term. This train travel really really fast. Not the fastest in the world. But if you have never travelled in a bullet train make it a your trip itinerary.

How much does it costs?

Can be slightly different depends on where you buy it. As a reference you can refer Buy JR Pass

I bought the 7 day nation wide pass for SGD 350+ during the April of 2015.

Should I buy it? Is it worth the price?

Of course it is not cheap. In simple terms if you plan to use Japan Shinkansen more than once, you definitely make make big savings by buying the JR Pass. Just choose the pass best match your trip itinerary.

Where can I buy it?

Not in Japan at the time of this writing. But keep a lookout Japan is working on making it available in major Airport for foreign tourists to purchase. For now buy it before you arrive Japan. You can buy online with some official distributors. The above link I shared under cost section is one of them.

Or you can buy them at physical locations of official partner travel agents. Just Google "Official JR Pass distributors" you will find some.

This may be useful

Can I use it for any Japanese train?

No. Pass is limited to use of trains operated by JR. There are other operators as well. For example subways are operated by other operators. But JR is covers most trains in Japan and most of the areas as well.

Can I use JR Pass on any Shinkansen?

Not again. There are some super express bullet trains that JR Pass is not valid. But there are fair enough number of Shinkansen's you are are allowed to travel with JR Pass.

Do I get reserved seat on Shinkansen when I travel with a JR Pass?

Not by default. But you are entitled to reserved seat. You can bring your Pass to counters at stations and exchange it to a ticket that will reserve you a seat.
Not that Shinkansen's have 3 types of cars.

  • Green Cars.
  • Reserved Cars.
  • Normal Cars.

Get in the right car.

How should I activate the pass.

Yes you need to activate the pass upon your arrival in Japan. Reach JR rail

How to use the pass.

At the time of this writing the JR Pass is only a printed ticket. So no access to turnstiles. You have to go to the fare booth at either side of the station gate and show your pass to JR employee to enter.

Again if you want to reserved seat go to ticket counter and reserve a seat advance. Reservation is only allowed for the same day.

Where can I find the Shinkansen time table.

Use HyperDia.com.

Where else I can use JR Pass.

Apart from being able to use any JR line across Japan JR pass offer additional benefits to tourists.
You can use it to travel in,

  • Narita Express.
  • JR Bus services. There are many JR buses.
  • JR Miyajima Ferry.

You Want to visit Japan for cherry blossom.

Well, who don't want to? Obviously you need little planning early. You get a 2-3 month window. But flowers only bloom for less than 2 weeks. Fully bloomed span is even less. So area is the key. Choose which area in Japan you visit wisely. I'm trying to share my experience with planning and viewing Sakura.

When Should I visit Japan to experience Sakura bloom?

It depends on place of visit. There are predictions for the bloom based on the area.
JNTO Cherry Blossom Predictions is a very good source to track predictions. Flowers only bloom for less than 2 weeks. You got to be lucky to plan a trip early for the full bloom. Typically, It begins on southern islands of Okinawa in late January. As temperature rises it move towards north.

Is hotels and air tickets expensive during the season?

Of course. Hotels can be fully booked if you wait until last week. So follow the predictions and plan early. Even Airbnb listings becomes expensive and rare during season.

What are the best locations?

Tokyo remains a very popular Sakura viewing destination. But all over the Japan is blanketed with flowers. Again choose the location based on the time you visit. Followings are some snaps I captured at Tokyo.
