Initial trip planning on Google Maps

05 May 2016

If you cud bring yourself to plan your next trip yourself, Google is your friend. Google rumored to developing a ultimate travel planning app at the time of writing. Until we have such luxury, we have to manage with existing tools and apps.

Google Maps

Look at the draft custom map I created for my recent trip. (Give it few seconds custom maps take its time to load)

You can see colored markers with clearly marked airports.

I kept bookmarking places of interest with google maps stars first. Well Japan is such a awesome country that I ended up with blanket of stars all over the map of country.
So I created a custom map with custom markers and icons.
Visit https://maps.google.com. Go to menu and select your places. Under map section you get a button create map. There you will get a map playground. Play with it you will easily figure it out.
